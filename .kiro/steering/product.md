# Product Overview

This is a multi-tenant restaurant management system built with Laravel. The platform provides a comprehensive SaaS solution for restaurants to manage their operations including:

## Core Features

- **Multi-tenant architecture** - Each restaurant operates as an isolated tenant with their own database
- **Point of Sale (POS)** - Cash register management with session tracking
- **Table management** - Visual table layout with order tracking and QR code generation
- **Menu management** - Product and category management with online menu display
- **Order processing** - Kitchen display system (KDS) with preparation area workflows
- **Delivery management** - Online ordering and delivery tracking
- **Stock control** - Inventory management and tracking
- **User management** - Role-based access for restaurant staff
- **Statistics & reporting** - Sales analytics and cash session reports

## Business Model

- **Central admin panel** - Super admin manages all restaurants, plans, and subscriptions
- **Tenant isolation** - Each restaurant has complete data separation
- **Subscription-based** - Plans with trial periods and feature modules
- **Modular features** - Tables and delivery modules can be enabled/disabled per restaurant

## Target Users

- Restaurant owners and managers
- Kitchen staff (via KDS)
- Waiters and cashiers (via POS)
- Customers (via online menu and ordering)
